Screwed-in Systems
24 Injection points, PP/PE-EPDM molding material
Injection system with nozzles screwed directly into the hot runner system

Having the nozzles screwed directly into the hot runner system ensure that this system avoids any potential problems from plastic material leakage in the event of installation error.

The hot runner and nozzles, heated by tubular heaters, are specially-designed to achieve optimal temperature control of the entire system and at the injection point. Another strength of the system is its ease of maintenance. Swift safe installation is guaranteed thanks to the 360-degree protection of all nozzle thermocouples and heaters.

Advantages
  • Standard Integrated Product
  • Significant adaptability if tailored solutions are required
