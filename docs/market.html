Automotive exterior
A product range aimed at satisfying the typical technical requirements of large component moulding.
High design flexibility for the mould maker and guarantee users with optimal reliability over time

The systems are designed to reduce reprocessing of the mould to a minimum, to safeguard the sturdiness of the mould itself. The systems are also designed to facilitate operations of assembly and maintenance.

HRSflow products also enable reduction of the axial clearance of systems, to enable a reduction of direct costs of the mould.

Your Benefits
  • Reduction in number of control zones
  • Reduction of discharges in mould
  • Ease of installation and maintenance (any lateral manifolds are wired independently from the main manifold)
  • Reduced axial clearance
