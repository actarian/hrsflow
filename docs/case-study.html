Customer's Expectations

Interior parts as door panel obviously require very high cosmetic standard and perfect surface quality. The customerâ€™s target for this application was to avoid important aesthetical defects such as pressure lines.

Our Solution

With the traditional sequential opening, an important pressure line appeared in the lower central area due to fast opening of needle M3.

Standard sequence pressure line

Aesthetical appearance was easily improved setting an optimized opening profile of the valve pin M3. The pressure line was definitely removed , as it is showed in FLEXFlow sequence.

FLEXFlow sequence pressure line removed
The hot runner system is composed by n. 7 nozzles Gp series with conical valve gate and through the cavity end rings.
Each nozzle is managed by a FLEXflow electric motor with lateral configuration.
